Software, Applications and Technology Integration

  • ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) Integration – Integrate your organization’s ERP information systems for planning, purchasing, inventory, sales, marketing, finance and human resources.
  • Logistics Control – Infinity Network Solutions will work with your logistics control software to integrate seamlessly into your network. Have immediate reports and data on your business by eliminating purchasing errors, improving production efficiency, integrating manufacturing and accounting and analyzing production costs more accurately.
  • POS (Point of Sale) – Infinity Network Solutions enables the integration of your Point of Sale system third-party solutions such as order and customer shopping portals and more.
